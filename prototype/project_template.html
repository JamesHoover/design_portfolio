Voting App

The Problem

The voting turnout for the younger generation is way too low. They lose interest and believe their vote does not make a difference or the process is too time-consuming or complex. They are not informed enough to want to make a decision or even know how. Voting is a relatively confusing/involved process which is not taught in schools. Knowing when you can vote and on what the issues are and staying informed on those issues is time consuming.

The Solution

Create a resource that makes research easier and all in one place. Allow the user to fill out a ballot and cast that ballot all from their own device in their own home.

The Result

Increased the percentage of voter participation by 20%. Reduced friction of voting. Made for a more pleasant voter experience.

Personas

Fred is a 24 year old. He is always connected. He has become used to instant gratification. He wants his voice to be heard. He also want to know he made an informed decision.

Research

Through user interview I developed the insight that when voting in person users feel pressured to complete their ballot and move on. I also got insight into how influenced users can be by their family or biased social media. Many userd have trouble dicerning facts finding unbiased sources.
